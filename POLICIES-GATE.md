# Policies And Upstream Terms Gate

Repository text is not legal approval. The business owner must obtain
jurisdiction-appropriate review before accepting customers.

| Gate | Owner | Required evidence | NO-GO condition |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Terms of Service | Business owner + counsel | Approved dated public document | Missing or unapproved |
| Privacy Policy | Privacy owner + counsel | Approved policy matching actual logs, processors, retention, deletion, and transfers | Claims conflict with operations |
| Acceptable Use Policy | Security owner + counsel | Approved AUP covering abuse, prohibited content, suspension, and investigation | Missing enforcement authority |
| Refund policy | Business owner + counsel | Approved eligibility, timing, unused-credit, and delivery language | Operator cannot apply consistently |
| Chargeback procedure | Finance owner | Processor-specific evidence and response SOP | No evidence retention or loss limit |
| Upstream resale rights | Business owner + counsel | Dated copy or written approval for each provider/model | Resale/proxying prohibited or unclear |
| Data-processing terms | Privacy owner + counsel | Processor/subprocessor list and required agreements | Required agreement missing |
| Vision provider disclosure | Privacy owner + counsel | Public disclosure of provider, region, image data processing, logging, retention, deletion, and transfer behavior | Image handling differs from public policy |

Do not accept payment, publish pricing, or promote the service while any row is
NO-GO. Re-check upstream terms before adding a provider or model and at least
quarterly.
